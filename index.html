Biography

I am a Staff Research Scientist at Meta Reality Labs working on 3D GenAI. Previously, I worked as a Senior Research Scientist at Amazon GenAI (2D/3D/Video) Evaluation Media, leading the 3D Foundation Model team to enhance the customer shopping experience. I also worked at Amazon Go Research at AWS, leading 3D human and object modeling and video action recognition projects. I obtained the Ph.D. in Computer Science at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, working with Prof. Derek Hoiem. I also worked closely with Prof. David Forsyth. Before that, I received the B.S. degree in Computer Science at Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Deng Cai.

My research interests lie in the areas of 3D computer vision. Specifically, my research focuses on 3D/Video Generative AI, Mixed view 3D Geometry, 3D Scene Understanding.

I was a summer intern student at University of California, Los Angeles in 2013, working with Prof. Alan Yuille on 3D scene estimation. I interned at Google DayDream (summer 2015), Adobe Research (summer 2016), Zillow 3D vision team (summer 2017) and Nvidia Research (summer 2018).
